About Us

Welcome to Neelima Ice Creams Parlour, your go-to destination for delicious ice creams, refreshing milkshakes, crunchy dry fruits, and tasty snacks! Whether you're craving a sweet treat or looking for the perfect addition to your home gatherings, we've got you covered. We take pride in offering high-quality ice creams, mouth-watering mocktails, fresh milk, creamy curd, soft paneer, and a variety of flavorful vegetarian and non-vegetarian pickles. Looking for something extra special? Explore our collection of imported chocolates that add a delightful touch to any occasion. Planning a celebration? We supply ice creams and snacks for home occasions, making your special moments even sweeter. Visit us today and indulge in the best flavors in town!
